Our office spaces are thoughtfully designed to offer you the perfect blend of functionality and flexibility. With lockable units admeasuring between approx. 1000 to approx. 1500 square feet, you will have the ideal canvas to shape your workspace according to your unique needs.

TOWER B

Tower B rises elegantly to 15 floors, featuring an expansive floor plate of approx. 53,000 square feet – one of the Noida’s largest. Setting a remarkable standard, it offers businesses a vast canvas for versatile office configurations.
21 high-speed elevators in the towers feature cutting-edge technology for enhanced efficiency and convenience, ensuring swift vertical movement and optimizing accessibility and comfort for all occupants.
